At Vinebase, we believe every direct purchase has the power to make a difference.

Here's how we do it:

Pricing should be fair & transparent.

With Vinebase, wineries get 100% of the proceeds of each sale. We only charge wineries a flat transaction fee of $2.95.

Support people, not conglomerates.

The impact is that wineries make 5-7x the profit, and you get higher quality wine for the price.

Quality wine should be a given.

A better wine industry starts with you.

Together, we can change the way we drink wine and support small businesses along the way.

Here are three easy steps to #DrinkBetter:

Shop your values - find wineries that support the same things you do. Whether that’s climate-conscious wineries, women-led wineries, minority-led wineries, or something else, Vinebase makes it easy to discover wineries that align with your values.

Buy direct - buy wine directly from wineries’ websites, their tasting rooms, or using Vinebase. When you order through Vinebase, 100% of the profits go directly to the winery.

Tell your friends - most people are unaware of the challenges faced by small wineries just to survive. They also don’t know that the wines they’re drinking from the Big Guys might contain large amounts of chemicals and be mass produced! Tell them to #DrinkBetter and think about the wine they buy.
