What the Big Guys don't want you to know.

It Hurts Diversity in the Industry

Between 1970 and 2019, one third of existing family-owned wineries in Napa & Sonoma were sold to corporate entities. 14% closed entirely.

Source: Jordan Winery

It Causes Huge Markups

Distributors and Retailers each expect a ~30% cut. oIf you buy a bottle for $40, they winery only receives $15. And because the price increases operate off percentages,markups are even bigger with higher quality wines..

Source: Wine Folly

It Limits Quality

The average Retail Shelf is 98% big brands who are industrially producing "bulk wine" where flavorings and chemicals outshine the wine itself.

Source: Cornell Law
